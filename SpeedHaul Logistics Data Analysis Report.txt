üìä 6. KPI Design and DAX Measures (Power BI)
Created DAX Measures
KPI				DAX Formula						Description
Total Deliveries	Total Deliveries = COUNT('Table'[DeliveryID])		Total number of delivery records
On-Time Deliveries	On-Time Deliveries = CALCULATE(COUNT('Table'[DeliveryID]), 'Table'[On-Time] = "Yes")	Total successful on-time deliveries
On-Time Rate (%)	On-Time Rate (%) = DIVIDE([On-Time Deliveries], [Total Deliveries]) * 100	Percentage of on-time deliveries
Average Delivery Time	Average Delivery Time = AVERAGE('Table'[Average Delivery Time (hrs)])	Average time per delivery
Average Client Satisfaction	Average Client Satisfaction = AVERAGE('Table'[Client Satisfaction])	Mean satisfaction rating
Total Fuel Cost (GHS)	Total Fuel Cost (GHS) = SUM('Table'[Fuel Cost])	Total fuel expenditure
Average Packages per Trip	Average Packages per Trip = AVERAGE('Table'[Packages Delivered])	Packages handled per delivery

 8. Key Insights

65% on-time delivery rate, below the ideal 90% benchmark.

Trucks TRK102 and TRK107 have the longest average delivery times.

Fuel costs increase significantly on certain mid-length routes.

Client satisfaction drops sharply when delivery times exceed 4 hours.

Fleet utilization is uneven ‚Äî some trucks are overworked.
